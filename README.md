# zeroGravity

zeroGravity is a futuristic engineering and drone media brand focused on:

- Drone cinematography
- Custom FPV drone builds
- AI-powered aerial robotics (future vision)

## Repository Purpose
This repository is the business, brand, and planning hub for zeroGravity.

## Structure
- `docs/01_business_plan/` – business plan, King’s Trust work, funding notes
- `docs/02_market_research/` – surveys, results, competitor research
- `docs/03_services/` – service offerings and packages
- `docs/04_operations/` – workflows, equipment, safety, delivery process
- `docs/05_brand/` – story, messaging, pitch
- `docs/06_roadmap/` – short and long-term growth plan
- `docs/07_cpd/` – learning and development log
- `docs/08_management/` – planning, admin, weekly execution
- `assets/` – visuals, charts, and supporting media

## Current Priority
Build and validate zeroGravity’s first-year drone cinematography service for property and event clients.

## Vision
To bridge imagination and reality through advanced drone technology.
